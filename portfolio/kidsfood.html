Wisconsin Kids Rely on School Lunch

Over 800,000 Wisconsin kids rely on schools for education, care, and nutrients. This map is a bivariate choropleth showing each county’s percent limited access to healthy foods and percent participation in free and reduced lunch. As you can see, children and families across Wisconsin lack adequate access to healthy foods and 330,000 students participate in fee and reduced lunch, indicating an enhanced need for those school lunched to be a healthy resource.
